# Privacy Policy

Last updated:  June 17, 2026

This app collects and stores information that users choose to provide in order to deliver the app's functionality.

## Information We Collect

We may collect and store:

- Account information, such as email address and authentication data.
- Content and information that users create or enter within the app.
- Technical and usage information about how the app is used.

## Analytics

We may collect analytics data to understand how the app is used, improve functionality, and enhance the user experience. Analytics data is collected in an aggregated or pseudonymized form where possible.

## Data Storage

User data is stored using Supabase, which provides authentication and database services for the application.

## Sharing of Information

We do not sell personal information to third parties.

We may share data with service providers that help us operate the app, including:

- Supabase (authentication and data storage)
- Analytics providers (such as Amplitude)

These providers process data on our behalf and according to their own privacy and security commitments.

## Data Retention

We retain user data for as long as necessary to provide the service or comply with legal obligations.
